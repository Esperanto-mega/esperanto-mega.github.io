    My name is Jun YIN. Currently, I am a PhD Student in the Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University under the supervision of Professor Chengqi ZHANG and Professor Shirui PAN. I received my Master degree in Electronic Information and Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Central South University in 2025 and 2022, respectively. From September 2021 to June 2025, I study in BigData 4 Urban Intelligence Lab lead by Professor Senzhang WANG. From October 2023 to February 2025, I worked as Onsite Research Intern at Microsoft AI supervised by Professor Chaozhuo LI, focusing on LLM-based sequential recommendation. From October 2023 to February 2025, I work as a Research Intern at Microsoft AI, focusing on LLM-based generative recommender system.

    My main research interests lie in

    • Large Language Model, Recommender Systems
    • Graph Neural Networks, GNN Explanation Methods, Interpretable GNNs
