About Me

Hello! I am a PhD Student in the Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Chengqi ZHANG and Prof. Shirui PAN.

I received my Master's degree in Electronic Information and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Central South University in 2025 and 2022, respectively. During my studies, I was a member of the BigData 4 Urban Intelligence Lab led by Prof. Senzhang WANG.

Previously, I worked as a Research Intern at Microsoft AI (Oct 2023 - Feb 2025) supervised by Chaozhuo LI, focusing on LLM-based generative recommendation.
